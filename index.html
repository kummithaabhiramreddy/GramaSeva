📋 Process

How GramaSeva Works

Simple, fast and built for every village household

1

Worker Registers

A skilled worker from your village registers individually or as a group. They enter their trade, experience, and village name.

2

Profile is Listed

The worker's profile appears on the platform, searchable only within their own village — keeping it local and trusted.

3

Customer Searches

A customer in the same village searches by trade and finds available workers or groups nearby.

4

Book & Confirm

The customer clicks "Book" on a worker's card, fills in job details, and confirms the booking instantly.
